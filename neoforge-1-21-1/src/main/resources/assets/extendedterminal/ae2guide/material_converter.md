# Material Converter

<ItemImage id="extendedterminal:material_converter" scale={3}/>

<ItemLink id="extendedterminal:material_converter" /> converts old Extended Terminal material items stored in an online AE2 network into their Myotus replacements.

Place it on the AE2 network and right-click the block to run the conversion.

## Converted Materials

- `extendedterminal:compat_processor` → <ItemLink id="myotus:compat_processor" />
- `extendedterminal:printed_compat_processor` → <ItemLink id="myotus:printed_compat_processor" />
- `extendedterminal:compat_press` → <ItemLink id="myotus:compat_press" />
- `extendedterminal:charged_ender_pearl` → <ItemLink id="myotus:charged_ender_pearl" />
